The Exynos W920 is Samsung’s newest offering with an advanced 5nm EUV process for the next generation of wearable devices. It packs two Arm® Cortex®-A55 cores for high-performing yet power-efficient processing and an Arm Mali™-G68 GPU that boasts improved CPU performance around 20-percent and ten times better graphics performance than its predecessor.
