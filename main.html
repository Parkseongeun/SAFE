Welcome to SAFETM (Samsung Advanced Foundry Ecosystem) Forum 2021.

Since 2018, Samsung Foundry has been operating the foundry ecosystem program called SAFE™ (Samsung Advanced Foundry Ecosystem) to ensure deep collaboration between ecosystem partners and customers and deliver competitive and robust System on Chip (SoC) designs based on △IP (Intellectual Property) △EDA (Electronic Design Automation) △ Cloud △ Design Service and △ Package.

In this virtual forum, we will have a lineup of incredible C-level keynotes and about 80 technical sessions covering the latest advancements of each field to help you design the next generation of performance platforms 2.0 : Innovation, Intelligence & Integration.

Register today to engage deeper with customers within Samsung's foundry ecosystem.
