Welcome to SAFE Forum 2021!

We will provide Samsung Foundry's latest technology status and application-specific solutions and strengthen partnership including Samsung Keynote, HPC, eNVM, RF, and CIS.

This forum consists of 3 parts. In the first part, Samsung Keynote will show you state-of-the-art foundry platforms that bring together essential technical elements for new-age applications, while expanding its manufacturing operations to improve global customers’ needs to Samsung’s foundry solutions. In the session part, 6 sessions will be provided to build one more dimension in your business including technological innovation, operational capability and total solutions. Also, you can make live discussion with Samsung and all attendees from over the world. Additionally, you can win a brand new Samsung Neo QLED TV if you complete missions to a raffle.

Hope you have a valuable time at SAFE Forum 2021!
