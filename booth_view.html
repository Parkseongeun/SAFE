Samsung

Overview

Samsung initiated a foundry business in 2005 and established Samsung Foundry as an independent business unit in 2017.

As a pure-play foundry provider, Samsung Foundry provides customers with a wide range of state-of-the-art process technologies, from EUV-based 3nm GAA and 4nm FinFET to 28nm FD-SOI, 8-inch 180nm, and other specialty technologies including RF, eNVM (eFlash and eMRAM), DDI and CMOS image sensors. These technologies enable a variety of new opportunities for emerging applications such as High Performance Computing (HPC), AI, 5G/mobile, and automotive. Aside from process technology, Samsung Foundry offers a full range of certified EDA/DM solutions, product-proven IPs, a flexible cloud design environment, comprehensive ASIC design services, as well as 2.5D/3D package and test services.

Samsung Foundry prides itself on working closely with every customer to achieve their market success. From the initial design concept to high-volume manufacturing, Samsung Foundry aims to be a partner with transparency and flawless execution at every stage. Samsung Foundry, along with our partners in the Samsung Advanced Foundry Ecosystem (SAFE™) program, empower fabless and IDM companies to bring their advanced innovative products to the world.
To learn more and contact us with your questions at: foundry@samsung.com
